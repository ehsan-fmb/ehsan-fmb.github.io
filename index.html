Ehsan Futuhi

I am an AI researcher working at planning algorithms including intersection of reinforcement learning, heuristic search, and foundation-model reasoning. I received my M.Sc. in Computing Science from the University of Alberta under the supervision of Prof. Martin Müller, where my research focused on improving the sample efficiency of policy gradient algorithms in sparse-reward continuous control tasks using search and subgoal discovery.

I am currently a Data Scientist at MedWatch Technologies, working on AI methods for non-invasive blood glucose monitoring. I am also a part-time Research Assistant at the Moving AI Lab under the supervision of Prof. Nathan Sturtevant, where I study the planning andreasoning capabilities of large language models in planning tasks.
